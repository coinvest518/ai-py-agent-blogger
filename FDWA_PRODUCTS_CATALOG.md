### Pricing Psychology:

**Free Lead Magnets (Always mention first):**
- Removes friction
- Builds trust
- Gets email/contact info
- Creates reciprocity

**Low-Ticket ($1-$10):**
- Easy impulse buys
- Perfect for templates/single guides
- Mention as "quick start" options

**Mid-Ticket ($25-$75):**
- Premium guides and template packs
- Emphasize member discounts ($50 → $25)
- Position as "complete solution"

**High-Ticket ($125-$350):**
- Courses and comprehensive training
- Mention ROI and transformation
- Always offer consultation alternative

**Products with Member Pricing:**
- How to Sue Debt Collectors ($125 → $75) - 40% off

## Performance Tracking (To Implement)

### Metrics to Track per Product:

- **Mention Count** - How many blogs mentioned this product?
- **Click-Through Rate** - How many times was the link clicked?
- **Conversion Rate** - How many purchases from blog mentions?
- **Revenue Generated** - Total revenue attributed to blog mentions
- **Best-Performing Topics** - Which blog topics drove most sales?

### Optimize Based on Data:

- **High CTR, Low Conv** - Pricing may be too high, or description needs work
- **Low CTR** - Link placement or integration needs improvement
- **High Conv** - Mention more often, create dedicated blog post
- **No Clicks** - Product not resonating, consider replacing with alternative
